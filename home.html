What is SHIMS-GH?

SHIMS-GH is a school health information system that supports early detection, crisis prevention, and coordinated care for students with Sickle Cell Disease in SHS boarding schools.

Core Features

Health Profiles

Digital SCD history, triggers & medication.

Daily Monitoring

Pain, hydration, temperature & symptoms.

Emergency Module

Alerts, crisis protocol & readiness tools.

Telemedicine

Nurse–doctor consultations & referrals.
